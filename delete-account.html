Request Account Deletion

This page provides instructions for users who wish to delete their account and associated data from Twenty Two Solutions.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at: admin@twnetytwosolutions.com

support@yourdomain.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered phone number or email address
  • A brief statement confirming that you want to delete your account

What Happens After You Request Deletion?

Once we verify your identity, we will delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days.

No personal data will be retained unless legally required (e.g., for security, fraud prevention, or legal obligations).

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at the email above.
